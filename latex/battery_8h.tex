\doxylink{class_battery}{Battery} monitoring system for LILYGO ESP32-\/based boards.

\begin{DoxyItemize}

\item Outlier removal for stable measurements
\item Safety voltage thresholds and automatic shutdown
\item Power management with light/deep sleep modes
\item \doxylink{class_battery}{Battery} percentage calculation for lithium batteries 
\end{DoxyItemize}
